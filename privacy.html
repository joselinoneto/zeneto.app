Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Just Football is built on a simple promise: it collects nothing. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs.

What we collect

Nothing. The app does not ask for your name, email, location, or any other personal information, and it never sends data about you anywhere.

How the app works

Just Football downloads the public schedule and results of the 2026 international football tournament over a secure connection and caches that data on your device so the app works offline. This data is about matches and teams — not about you.

Analytics & tracking

There are none. The app contains no analytics frameworks, advertising identifiers, or third-party tracking SDKs of any kind.

Data sharing

Because no personal data is collected, there is nothing to share, sell, or disclose to anyone.

Children's privacy

The app is safe for all ages. As it collects no personal information, it collects none from children either.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
